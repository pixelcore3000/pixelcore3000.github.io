Assignments

There will be 11 weekly assignments, all prompts to create a new webpage and host it using github desktop. Assignments are due by 8AM, before class. Each sketch is worth 5 points, based on the following:

  • 1 pt- Did you make a web page?
  • 2 pt- Did you make a substantive web page?
  • 3 pt- Does your web page clearly respond to the assignment criteria?
  • 4 pt- Does your web page demonstrate understanding of the technical topics presented in the assignment criteria and weekly demos?
  • 5 pt- Is your code properly indented and readable?

Grading

The following grading rubric will be used to assess both mid-semester score and final grade.

  • 55% Assignments
  • 15% In-Class Exercises
  • 10% Core 2 Skills Test
  • 20% Class Participation

Across the semester, 11 weekly assignments will be worth 55pts. In class exercises will be worth 15pts. There will be a final skills test worth 10pts. Participation is worth 20pts and is weighed against frequency (regular contribution to critiques, enthusiasm for subject, and quantity of comments) and professionalism (timely and productive weekly progress, attitude to feedback, verbal and visual presentation of process and final work). In total, there are 100 points. Grades: A (95 – 100); A- (90 – 94); B+ (86 – 89); B (84 – 85); B- (80 – 83); C+ (76 – 79); C (74 – 75); C- (70 – 73); D (60 – 69); F (59 and below)
